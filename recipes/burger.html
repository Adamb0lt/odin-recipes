Burger recipe

HomePage

Description

A burger is a classic grilled sandwich made with a juicy beef patty served inside a soft bun and topped with cheese, vegetables, and condiments. It’s simple, hearty, and customizable, making it one of the most popular comfort foods worldwide.

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter or oil

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook patties 3–4 minutes per side.
  4. Add cheese and let it melt.
  5. Toast buns lightly with butter.
  6. Assemble burger with toppings and condiments.
  7. Serve hot.
