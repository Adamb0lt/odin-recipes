Tacos recipe

HomePage

Description

Tacos are a flavorful handheld dish made with seasoned meat, fresh toppings, and soft or crispy tortillas. They are vibrant, quick to prepare, and packed with bold taste.

  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Taco seasoning
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook meat until browned.
  3. Add taco seasoning and stir.
  4. Warm tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  5. Fill tortillas with meat.
  6. Add toppings and cheese.
  7. Serve immediately.
